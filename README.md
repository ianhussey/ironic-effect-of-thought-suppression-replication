# Ironic effect of thought suppression

This repo contains data for Wang et al.'s RRR of the ironic effect of thought suppression (forthcoming). 

Malte Elson and I originally participated in this RRR. We and our RAs collected data at two sites in Germany and Switzerland. 

Malte and I developed serious concerns about the governance and execution of the RRR and the accruacy of claims made in the drafts of the manuscript that we were sent. Once we felt we could not resolve these concerns with the organising team or journal, we withdrew our names as coauthors of Wang et al. We will write a commentary on Wang et al. in time.
