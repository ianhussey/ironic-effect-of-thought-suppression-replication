After discussion with the organisers, it was agreed that in study 2, the two new exploratory categories (food and disease) should be scored as "mentioned" by the participant if they talked about things related to the category and not just the use of the word itself. This refers to whether a "broad" or "narrow" scoring strategy should be used, and Wang et al. indicated it should be broad. We also scored it narrowly, to assess the impact of this on scores and their reliability (see the _narrow_scoring version of the file).
